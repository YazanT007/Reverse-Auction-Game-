Select the Level Difficulty

Easy Mode
A calm introduction to reverse auctions. Suppliers bid casually with no strategy. High reserve prices and no relationship effects make this a low-pressure way to learn the game.
Normal Mode
Suppliers react to buyer reputation and early relationship cues. With lower reserve prices, competition gets tighter—strategy starts to matter.
Hard Mode
Real-world pressure. Aggressive bids based on detailed relationships and buyer reputation. Low reserve prices make every move count—winning isn’t just about the lowest bid.
